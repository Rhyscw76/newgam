What is Shell Shockers?
Shell Shockers is a popular multiplayer IO game that puts players in the role of armed eggs engaged in intense egg-themed battles. In this fast-paced shooter game, players control egg characters armed with various types of firearms, such as shotguns and rifles. The objective is to eliminate opponents while trying to stay alive.

Shell Shockers offers different game modes, including Free For All, Teams, and Capture the Spatula. Players can choose from different classes of eggs, each with its own unique weapon and abilities. The game's quirky and humorous art style adds to its charm.

The gameplay of Shell Shockers is fast, frantic, and filled with egg-pun humor. Players must aim precisely and move strategically to outwit and outshoot their opponents. The game's simple controls make it accessible to players of all skill levels.

As players earn points and score kills, they can level up and unlock additional weapons, gear, and cosmetic customization options. Shell Shockers also features leaderboards, allowing players to compete for the top rankings and showcase their egg-shooting prowess.

Whether engaging in chaotic battles or teaming up with friends, Shell Shockers offers an enjoyable and addictive multiplayer experience that combines humor, egg-themed action, and competitive gameplay.

How Do I Play Shell Shockers?
WASD to move
Space to Jump
E to change weapons
Q to launch grenade
Left Mouse button to shoot
Shift to aim
R to reload
F to melee
